what is it..?

A computer needs something to power it. this device is responsible for supplying power to the computer is the power supply.it converts the alternating voltage also knowm as AC to electrical power system into continuous voltage also known as DC.in other words,it's converting conventional 110V or 220V alternating voltage into continuous voltage used by the PC electronic components, which are +3.3 V, +5 V, +12 V and -12 V. The power supply also relates the temperature of the computer.

if we didn't have it...?

if we didn't have a power supply in a computer it wouldn't work properly, overheating, not being able to charge and wouldn't last as long as if we did have it.
